About iCare Hospital

Established in 2005, iCare Hospital is one of the most trusted healthcare providers in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh. Our hospital is known for delivering compassionate and advanced medical care across multiple specialties including cardiology, orthopedics, pediatrics, and ophthalmology. With a dedicated team of doctors, nurses, and support staff, we provide high-quality healthcare services to more than 100 patients every day.

Our mission is to combine cutting-edge technology with compassionate care to improve patient health outcomes.
